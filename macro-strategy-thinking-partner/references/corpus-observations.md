# Corpus Observations

This file distills the currently downloaded Quark transcript corpus into reusable observations.

## 04 - 2024 Technology Strategy

- In fragile markets, technology can still become a core focus if valuation compression is already deep enough.
- Watch systemic-risk testing, foreign-flow behavior, and policy-event timing rather than relying on narrative alone.
- The transcript repeatedly implies: when everyone only sees risk, deeply compressed strategic sectors can become the medium-term battleground.

## 06 - Healthcare 2024 Strategy

- When the market is in a "financial protection" phase, asking "which sector is best" is often the wrong first question.
- Healthcare should be judged in the context of policy support, risk appetite, and whether the market is rewarding defense or growth.
- Good industry logic still needs the macro environment to cooperate.

## 18 - Quant for Ordinary Investors

- Quant is presented as an implementation layer and decision aid, not a substitute for macro judgment.
- Good execution tools improve discipline, monitoring, and consistency after the thesis is already built.
- The user should use quant to refine Layer 3, not to replace Layer 1 or Layer 2.

## 29 - AI and Large-Model Industry Trend

- Avoid binary conclusions like "the theme is finished" just because the price retraced.

- Theme heat is not the same as durable industrial transmission.

## 30 - Quant Index Enhancement and Risk Events
