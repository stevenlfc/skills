# Period Brief

This skill covers the `pre-924` corpus only.

Use it when the user is asking from the earlier regime where:

- market pressure is still heavy
- policy expectation exists, but the later 924-style shift has not happened yet
- technology and healthcare are discussed as compressed but important strategic directions
- rates, insurance, and household allocation logic appear as part of long-cycle thinking
- quant is mostly an execution aid, not the thesis core

Do not use this period brief for:

- later 924-after market framing
- 2025 or 2026 quarterly market outlook language
- later market-level slogans such as 3800 or 4000
