# Period Brief

This skill covers the `post-924 plus 2026` corpus only.

Use it when the user is asking from the later regime where:

- the market discussion has already crossed the 924 divide
- quarterly A-share outlook and market-level framing become more explicit
- policy transmission, liquidity follow-through, and risk appetite shifts are central
- 2025 and 2026 materials continue the same later-period line of thinking

Do not use this period brief for:

- earlier pre-924 compression-only discussions
- old-cycle insurance or healthcare framing without the later regime context
- answering as if the earlier and later periods were interchangeable
